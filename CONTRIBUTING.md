## General Guidelines

All repositories follow these shared conventions:

- **Commit messages**: Use [Conventional Commits](https://www.conventionalcommits.org/) format (`feat:`, `fix:`, `docs:`, etc.)
- **Pull requests**: Target the `development` branch (or `main` where no develop branch exists)
- **License**: All contributions are made under the [Apache 2.0 License](https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0)
- **Code of Conduct**: All participants are expected to follow our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
